RESULT PER STEP:

================================================================================
                         ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
================================================================================

EXCESSIVE SMALL TRADES CAUSED BY:

1. MIN NOTIONAL TOO LOW ($10)
   в”ңв”Җ $1,000 allocation bot
   в”ңв”Җ 1% rebalancing change = $10 swing
   в””в”Җ With $10 minimum вҶ’ trade executes!

2. REBALANCING EVERY STEP (60 seconds)
   в”ңв”Җ Score changes every bar (noisy on 1m)
   в”ңв”Җ Allocations recalculated every step
   в”ңв”Җ Cascades to position size changes
   в””в”Җ Forces many micro-trades

3. SIGNAL FLAPPING (No confirmation)
   в”ңв”Җ Mean Reversion oscillates near MA
   в”ңв”Җ Breakout hits new high/low every 5-10 bars on 1m
   в”ңв”Җ TrendFollow flips between MA crossovers
   в””в”Җ Each flip = potential trade

4. NO TRADE COOLDOWN
   в”ңв”Җ Same bot can trade multiple times per minute
   в”ңв”Җ No frequency limits
   в”ңв”Җ Buy/sell/buy cycles possible
   в””в”Җ Especially with oscillating strategies

5. 1-MINUTE TIMEFRAME TOO FAST
   в”ңв”Җ Strategies tuned for 5m/1h
   в”ңв”Җ At 1m, far more false signals
   в”ңв”Җ Micro price moves trigger rebalancing
   в””в”Җ Score bounces around constantly

6. 81 BOTS = 81 INDEPENDENT REBALANCING TARGETS
   в”ңв”Җ 27 per strategy manager
   в”ңв”Җ 3 symbols Г— 3 parameter variants
   в”ңв”Җ Each step, all 81 positions recalculated
   в””в”Җ More bots = more allocation churn
